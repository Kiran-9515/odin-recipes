Poori Masala recipe

A simple and easy potato mashed curry prepared as a side dish to poori or for dosa. it is typically served in a fast food restaurant as a side dish to poori for morning breakfast. it is very simple dish made by mashing the boiled potatoes.

Ingredients of Roasted Poori Masala:

  • 5 potato / aloo
  • 3 tsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • ½ tsp cumin / jeera
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • Pinch hing / asafoetida
  • Few curry leaves
  • 1 chilli (slit)
  • 1 inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp gram flour / besan
  • 1¼ cup water
  • 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

How to make Poori Masala step-by-step guide:

  1. Firstly, heat 3 tsp oil and splutter 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp chana dal, ½ tsp cumin, 1 tsp urad dal, pinch hing, few curry leaves, 1 chilli and 1 inch ginger.
  2. Saute and splutter on medium flame.
  3. Further, add 1 onion and saute well. do not brown the onions.
  4. Additionally, add ¼ tsp turmeric and saute for a minute.
  5. Now add mashed potato, ½ tsp salt and mix well.
  6. In a small bowl dissolve 1 tbsp besan in ¼ cup water.
  7. Mix to smooth and lump free batter. pour the besan mixture over aloo.
  8. Also, add 1 cup water and mix well.
  9. Cover and cook for 10 minutes or until the besan is cooked well.
  10. After 10 minutes the aloo bhaji will thicken, so adjust consistency as required.
  11. Furthermore add 2 tbsp coriander, 1 tsp lemon juice and mix well.
  12. Finally, enjoy poori masala with hot poori or chapati.
